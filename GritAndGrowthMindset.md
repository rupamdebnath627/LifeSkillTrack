## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control


#### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal locus of control refers to the degree to which you believe you have control over your life.   

The key points in the video are: 
- The video explains us about internal vs external locus of control.
- People who believe in external locus of control believe that success only depends on whether you smart enough and external conditions are favorable.
- These kinds of people will often blame external conditions for their failure.
- The other kind of people are the ones who believe in internal locus of control. These people believe that success primarily depends on the hard work you have put in.
- The internal locus of control becomes their motivation to go even when everything is working against them.
- To succeed in life focus on the things you can control, i.e. hard work and not blame things that are beyond your control.
- To adapt internal locus of control start with solving problems in your life and learn to appreciate your efforts.
